Bio

WormRose, a Swedish hard rock/metal band with a female front, was established in 2022 by Nils Leufvenius, the band's composer and guitarist. The formation of the group took place when all four members crossed paths at the same rock music school.

Although Nils had initiated WormRose as a side project some time ago, the need for a team of accomplished musicians led him to recruit Zelda (vocals) and Adam Kapusta (drums). Their shared musical preferences made them enthusiastic contributors to the project.

Recognizing the need for an exceptional bassist, the band extended an invitation to Alex Flygare. Having previously known and played with two of the members, he gratefully accepted and joined, marking a fortuitous addition to WormRose. With a complete lineup, WormRose immersed themselves in recording their debut album and outlined plans for future releases, kicking off with the debut single, "Sinners are Shades." Since their formation, WormRose has released four songs, performed at various concerts, and has ambitious plans to unveil in 2024.

WormRose possesses the fortunate ability to seamlessly amalgamate four distinct personalities and a plethora of influences, resulting in a rock sound that stands apart from the ordinary. The diverse musical backgrounds of each member manifest in various facets and tones, reflecting influences from iconic bands such as Megadeth, Avenged Sevenfold, Cassiopeia, and Heart.

While consistently anchored in the realm of hard rock, WormRose's music gracefully navigates the amalgamated musicianship of the band, producing a captivating soundscape. This dynamic interplay adds an intriguing dimension to their sonic expression, creating an engaging and distinctive musical experience.

The band constantly experiments to achieve their artistic vision of integrity and uniqueness in lyrics and music. They aim to take listeners and the audience into sounds and environments where they are surprised by innovation and pure skill.
